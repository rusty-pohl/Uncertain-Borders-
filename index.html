Welcome to Midnight Lantern Press

Midnight Lantern Press publishes adult fiction that explores the depths of human connection and desire. Our Stories After Dark series delves into themes of love, obsession, chance encounters, and the uncertain borders between desire and reality. Published from Tokyo, Japan, each work is crafted with literary intention and emotional authenticity.
